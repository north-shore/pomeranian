PROCESS


We are open for calls from Monday to Friday from 9:30 AM to 9:30 PM (EST) and from 10 AM to 6 PM (EST) on Saturday.

Whatsapp: +1(919) 714-9161
You may send us an email to info@pomeranianbokennel.com   or leave us a voicemail at all hours.

We only display the puppies we have available. You can make your selection from our wide variety of breeds and coat colors by browsing each of our available puppies pictures and videos. We will deliver your puppy to your closest International Airport where Pet Cargo is available. Transportation cost by Pet Cargo is on us for the following mentioned airports: LAX, MIA, SFO, SEA, DFW, JFK, YYZ, and YVR.

    An inflight nanny is required if direct Pet Cargo flights are not available to your city. The inflight nanny cost can vary from a destination to another. We do NOT ship to Australia, South Africa, Hawaii, or India unfortunately due to the rules and regulations on dog imports for entering these countries. We do not offer Pet Cargo service outside the USA or Canada.


 Purchasing Steps

Carefully select your teacup puppy from the  DOGS FOR SALE  section.

Contact us straight away with any further questions and concerns by calling  or send us an email to info@pomeranianbokennel.com  .

Once you have made your choice, contact us to book a live video call with the puppy you are interested in by calling WhatsApp: +1(919) 714-9161 or by sending us an email to info@pomeranianbokennel.com   
We ask for a $1000 deposit to hold your selected puppy if you are not paying in full. A deposit will reserve your puppy for (7) calendar days only.

When the (7) calendar days have passed and the balance owing on your puppy has not been paid, the buyer loses the initial deposit. If you wish to discuss a different payment arrangement, please call us  WhatsApp: +1 (919) 714-9161. 

Financing is available for US and Canadian-based clients only. If the final balance is not paid on the approved date within 7 days, the puppy will be placed back as available on the site. We accept the following payment options:PayPal by checkout via our site shopping cart or by sending payment to our PayPal ID (ask us for our PayPal ID) –  6% admin. **Please note that you can checkout via PayPal as a guest without needing to have a PayPal account.Bank wire $40 - $50 fee (depending on who you bank with) - Ask us to send you an email containing the bank wire information.


Once full payment has been made and the funds are available in our account, we will proceed with your puppy’s export process and flight booking. The export process can vary from 1-2 weeks depending on flight availability at the time of booking and on the weight of the puppy at the time of booking. Please note that a puppy needs to be between 1.5-2 lbs to be able to make the flight as per airline rules. Feel free to contact us for any further assistance before or after you’ve received your puppy. We are always available to ensure customer satisfaction.Please note that the nanny service attracts a separate charge and may vary from city to city.
